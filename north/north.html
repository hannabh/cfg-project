North London

Get ready for Camden!


Previous Next

Morning: Wonder around Camden Lock

Camden market is an amazing place where you can get lost no time. Its narrow streets filled with quirky little shops will keep you going all day. Visit the Indian and Vintage shops before going to the Rave shop where you can buy neon clothes for your next party!


Afternoon: Food of the world under the sunlight

Enjoy a good rest after the endless walking in the market and grab various different snacks at the international food stalls: from Vietnamese, to Hungarian and to French, you will not regret it. If you are lucky, find a sunny spot and enjoy your delicious lunch with your friends or family.


Evening: Get ready for Blue's Kitchen

Blue's Kitchen is a unique spot in London. A true American bar where they serve amazing burgers and cocktails in a stunning decor. Live bands will keep you dancing all night. If you get bored, Koko, the club in an old Italian opera, will wake you up you with 80s music!
